About

Pshdo (shoo-doo) is centered around PowerShell, Microsoft’s awesome task automation framework and command shell. The name is a play on the Unix sudo command.

Owned and operated by Aaron Jensen, a Configuration Management/DevOps/Build Architect in Portland, Oregon. I’ve been writing software since 1998 and automating everything he can since 2009. He created and maintains the Carbon and Pest open-source PowerShell modules.

You can e-mail him at aaron@splatteredbits.com or follow him on Twitter.
